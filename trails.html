SUGARLOAF MOUNTAIN HIKING


Sugarloaf Mountain has over 17 miles of trails across seven main sections. There are four trails, Blue, Purple, White, and Yellow, which are circuit hikes around the base and rising up to the summit of Sugarloaf. Several more sections, Green, Orange, and Red veer off and make runs to the summit itself from the curcuit hike sections.

A terrain map, powered by Google is shown above to display the elevation changes around Sugarloaf and below is a downloadable trail map for more details. If you forget to print a map, hard copies can be found at various map boxes at trailheads around the mountain.
